Frequently asked questions

Why does PottyMap need my location?

PottyMap maps your walking route and lets you claim territory, both of which rely on your device's location while a walk is active. You can manage this in your device's privacy settings.

How does claiming territory work?

As you walk, you can mark spots to claim grid cells on the map. Claims last for a limited time, so revisiting an area helps you hold your ground.

How do I delete my account?

Email us at pottymap@gmail.com and we'll remove your account and associated data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Is PottyMap free?

Yes — PottyMap is free to use.
