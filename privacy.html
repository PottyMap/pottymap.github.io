This Privacy Policy explains how PottyMap ("we," "us," or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the PottyMap mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a username. Authentication is handled through Amazon Web Services (AWS) Cognito.

Location data

PottyMap is a location-based app. With your permission, we collect your device's location while you record a walk so we can map your route and let you claim territory. You can revoke location access at any time in your device settings, though core features will not work without it.

Walk and activity data

We store the walks you record, the territory cells you claim, and related activity (titles earned, walk stories, chat messages, and similar content you create in the app).

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core features: route tracking, territory claiming, and standings.
  • To operate social features such as walk stories and chat.
  • To maintain, secure, and improve the app.

How Your Information Is Stored

Account, walk, and social data are stored on Amazon Web Services infrastructure (Amazon RDS and related services) in the United States.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. Certain content you create — such as your username, claimed territory, and walk stories — may be visible to other PottyMap users by design. We share data with service providers (such as AWS) only as needed to operate the app, and when required by law.

Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your information for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

PottyMap is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
