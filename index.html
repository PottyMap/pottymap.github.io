Features

More than a step counter

Every walk earns territory, memories, and points. Here's what you can do.

🐾

Mark every spot

On a walk, mark where your dog pees and poops. Add a photo and a note — keep it as a private memory or post it to the community.

🗺️

Claim your territory

Every mark claims a grid cell on the map. See exactly where your territory is, revisit favorite spots, and rank against nearby dogs.

📸

Share to the community

Post your walk photos to the feed — public for everyone or just for friends. Browse what other dogs are up to around you.

❤️

Like, comment & connect

React to posts, leave comments, and see who's viewing your photos. Every walk is a little moment worth sharing.

💬

Chat with friends

Add other walkers as friends and message them directly. Find walking buddies and keep up with the dogs in your area.

🏆

Earn points & rewards

Earn points hourly for top-ranked territories. Spend them on walk-snack cards, name a neighborhood, or boost a post.

Your dog's profile

Every walk, remembered

Your profile keeps it all in one place — the spots you've marked, the territory you hold, and the memories you've captured along the way.

  • Health, territories, and walk history at a glance
  • A photo gallery of every walk you've shared
  • Likes, comments, and views on each post
